Frequently Asked Questions

Whether “LOST REPORT” can be retrieved or not?

Yes, it can be retrieved through search link on the module.

Whether “LOST REPORT” can be lodged if article/document is lost outside the area of residence?

Yes, the case will be transferred to the concerned police station.

What types of complaints can be lodged?

This service can be used to lodge a 'LOST REPORT' for any article/document lost.

What action will be taken if complainant lodged any false report?

False complaint/information to the police is a punishable Offence .

I am a foreigner. Can I lodge a 'LOST REPORT' online?

You can lodge a police report through this application if you have lost or misplaced any article/document in India.

How I can report FOUND information of more than one item?

Repeat the process for multiple Found Article entries.

Is any investigation/enquiry done by police after submitting the FOUND Article?

Yes, the local police will verify and contact the concerned person who has reported the found article.

Is any investigation/enquiry done by police after lodging of a complaint?

This is only information to police. The Police will not investigate or make any enquiries on the information lodged in this Application but will only keep a record of it for future reference by any person or agency

Whether the print out of report taken is to be submitted in any police station?

No, it is not necessary, as one of the main purpose of this site is to promote digitalization.
